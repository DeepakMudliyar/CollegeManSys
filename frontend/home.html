Why Choose Deepak and Vijay College?

Excellence in quality education is developed keeping in mind the current needs of higher education. Our team of competent and qualified teaching faculty ensure focus on latest teaching pedagogics and bridge the gap between curriculum and industry requirements. The teaching methods vary from time to time and include a mixture of traditional chalk-duster- board as well as the use of smart boards and ICT. Research is also encouraged amongst the students by organising various expert sessions and other activities.
